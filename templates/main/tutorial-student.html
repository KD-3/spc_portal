 Student Tutorial for Website

For Students


Step 1:

  1. Click on Student Registration link given in the Login Page.


Step 2:

  1. Fill Out the following Registration Page that opens up.



Step 3:

  1. One can upload the Resumes by clicking on the "Upload Resume" tab in the navbar.


Step 4:

  1. Also one can check the status of the Resume, whether it is approved or not in "Upload Resume" tab itself.


Step 5:

  1. One can view the job/internship offers which are active and can also apply for the same from "View Offers" tab.
