 Recruiter Tutorial

For Companies


Step 1:

  1. Click on Company Registration link given in the Login Page.


Step 2:

  1. Fill Out the following Registration Page that opens up.



Step 3:

  1. One Can create a Job/Internship offer by clicking on the "Add Job/Intern Offer" tab on the navbar.


One can also specify the Selection Criteria and Procedure for the offer in the form itself.



Step 4:

  1. One Can View the job/Intern Offers created by cliking on "View Job/Intern offer" tab on the Navbar.

  2. One can also see the number of Applicants on the particular offer and also their Resumes.
